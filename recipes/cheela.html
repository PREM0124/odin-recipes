How to make Cheela

Description

A Healthy Breakfast which not only is full of flavours but also keeps you energized for whole day.

Ingredients

  • Basen(Gram flour) -> 100gm
  • Onion -> 1 quantity
  • Tomato -> 1 quantity
  • egg -> 1 quantity
  • Green Chilli -> 2-3 quantity
  • Salt -> 1 table spoon
  • Red chilli Powder -> 1 table spoon
  • Turmeric -> 1/2 table spoon

Steps to make Cheela

  1. Chop onions,tomatoes and green chillies into small pieces.
  2. Take a container and add basen, finely chopped onions, tomatoes, green chillies, one egg, salt, Red chilli powder and turmeric.
  3. Now add water in it slowly and make a thick consistensy batter of it
  4. Now take a frying pan and heat it on a medium flame.
  5. Put olive oli on the frying pan and than pour the batter on the pan and spread it, and cover it with lid
  6. After 1-1.5 mins take off the lid and filp the cheela so that it gets cooked on both side.
  7. Now your Cheela is ready ,Garnish it with coriender leavesa and serve it with tomato ketchup or green chutney.
