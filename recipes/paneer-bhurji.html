How to make Paneer bhurji

Description

Paneer Bhurji is a quick and flavorful Indian dish made with crumbled paneer sauteed with onions, tomatoes, spices, and herbs. It's perfect as a filling for rolls or served with roti or toast.

Ingredients

  • Paneer 200gm(crumbled)
  • Onion -> 2 quantity
  • Tomato -> 2 quantity
  • Green Chilli -> 2-3 quantity
  • Salt -> 1 table spoon
  • Red chilli Powder -> 1 table spoon
  • garam masala 1 table spoon
  • Turmeric -> 1/2 table spoon

Steps to make Paneer Bhuji

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Saute onions until golden brown.
  3. Add green chilies and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the crumbled paneer and mix gently to coat with the masala.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Add garam masala and mix.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
