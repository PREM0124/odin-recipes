How to make Poha

Description

A Recipe that will make your tasting buds explode with a lots of falvours is what Poha is all about which takes up about 20 min to make.

Ingredients

  • Poha(250gm)
  • Onion(2 quantity)
  • Tomato(2 quantity)
  • Potato(1 quantity)
  • Lemon(1 quantity)
  • Coriender leaves(7-8)
  • Peanuts(1/2 cup)
  • Green Chilli(2-3 quantity)
  • Salt(2 table of spoon)
  • Red chilli Powder(1.5 table of spoon)
  • Turmeric(1 table of spoon)

Steps to make Poha

  1. Soak the poha in water in some container, and after soaking remove the water to get the clean poha and let it dry for 2-3mins.
  2. Now add salt and sugar in to the poha.
  3. roast the peanuts in frying pan.
  4. Chop onions,tomatoes and green chillies into small pieces.
  5. Take a Deep frying pan set it to medium flame and add 2table spoons of olive oil in it.
  6. After the oli gets hot add onions in it and stir it for 2 mins. After that add Potatoes, tomatoes, green chillies and turmeric powder in it and mix them well and cover it with the lid for 3-4 min.
  7. Now take of the lid and add poha, red chilli powder, roasted peanuts and lemon juice in it and cook it for 2-3 min.
  8. Garnish the poha with coriender leaves. And your Poha is ready.
